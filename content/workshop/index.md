## ELEKIN Research Days  


The ELEKIN Research Days 2025 will take place in December 2025, bringing together researchers, students, industry partners, and collaborators to share recent advances and ongoing work within the field of intelligent and socially responsible engineering.

This annual meeting serves as a platform to showcase the multidisciplinary research carried out by the ELEKIN Group and its partners, promoting dialogue, collaboration, and innovation across different areas of engineering and technology.

The event will feature contributions and discussions around four core areas:

- Round tables and thematic discussions
